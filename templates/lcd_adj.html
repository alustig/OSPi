LCD settings

This plugin sends data to I2C for LCD 16x2 char with PCF8574. Visit for more: www.pihrt.com/elektronika/258-moje-rapsberry-pi-i2c-lcd-16x2.

Use I2C LCD:
Adress for PCF8574: (default 0x20)
Range adress: PCF8574 = 0x20-0x27, PCF8574A = 0x38-0x3F
Status:

Compatible with HD44780 LCD 16x2 controller.
